ABOUT AUTHOR:

BYRON ANTHONY D. CABLAY studied in O.B. Montessori Center from kindergarten to Highschool. After grade 10, he went to University of Santo Thomas to continue his senior highschool up until college when he majored in BS in Pharmacy. His academic journey was just like the others'-getting great grades, starting friendships, ending friendships, breakdowns, and such. But all of that will not stop him from succeeding in life. He was able to produce many novels that caught the attention of many. When he noticed his talent of writing, he thought of using this for the good of many. And so he wrote this blog!
